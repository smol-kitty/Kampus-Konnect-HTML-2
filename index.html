The Student Startup & Innovation Policy (SSIP) Cell at The Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda is dedicated to fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and startup culture among students. It provides a structured framework for students to submit proposals for incubation, funding, and intellectual property rights (IPR) support. Through SSIP, students can transform their innovative ideas into real-world solutions with financial assistance, mentorship, and industry exposure. The cell actively supports projects under PoC (Proof of Concept), IPR, and prototyping, helping students bridge the gap between ideation and execution.
